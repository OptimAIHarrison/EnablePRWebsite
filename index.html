Why PR?

Good PR isn't a nice-to-have.
It's essential.

In a market this loud, being good at what you do isn't enough — people need to know you're good at it. PR is how credibility gets built, how trust gets earned, and how a name becomes a household name.

FAQ

Questions worth asking before you start.

The honest answers to what people want to know about working with enablePR.

Startups and new product launches, growing brands ready to dominate their category, founders and executives building a personal brand, and public figures or artists with a story worth telling are all ready for PR. If you've got something worth saying, you're ready to say it to the right audience.

PR builds credibility through earned media — coverage from trusted, independent publications — which carries more weight than advertising because it's a third-party endorsement. Good PR helps a business or founder become a recognised name in their industry, build trust with customers and investors, and create lasting visibility rather than one-off impressions.

enablePR is led directly by founder Kane Lewis, meaning every client gets direct access to an experienced publicist on every campaign — no account managers, no junior teams, no diluted strategy. You get the same calibre of media relationships and results as a large agency, at freelance rates rather than a $10,000+ monthly retainer.

Kane Lewis is the founding director of enablePR, an independent PR and communications consultancy based in Australia.
